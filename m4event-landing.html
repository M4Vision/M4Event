FAQ

Questions answered.

What is M4Event?

M4Event is a mobile-first event management platform. Create events, sell tickets with QR codes, manage attendees, and share photos — all from one app.

Is it free to use?

Creating free events is free. For paid events, M4Event takes a small service fee per ticket sold. No hidden costs, no monthly subscriptions.

How does QR ticketing work?

Every ticket gets a unique QR code. Attendees show it at the door, you scan it with your phone — instant contactless check-in. Works offline too.

Can I host private events?

Yes. Set any event to private and only invited guests can see details, RSVP, and receive tickets. Perfect for corporate events, weddings, or exclusive gatherings.

What platforms are supported?

M4Event is available on iOS and Android. Download from the App Store or Google Play. A web version is coming soon.

How do I get started?

Download the app, create an account, and set up your first event in under 5 minutes. No technical skills required.
